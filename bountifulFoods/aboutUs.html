Learn More About Us!

We were founded in 1975 in Carlsbad California after our founder Richard Bountiful ate a very sour lemon and thought, "Why arn't all lemons this sour". He then made it his mission to create the worlds most consistantly sour lemon and experimented on many different fruits along the way. Eventually his lemon passion project turned into a profitable produce supply business. He may not have created the most sour lemon, but here at Bountiful Foods we look up to our founder as a good man with good principles and strive to live up to his example of hard work and persistence.

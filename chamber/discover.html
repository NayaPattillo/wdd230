The Museum of the Rockies

In 2022 we had 209,406 visitors to our museum, 330,000 visitors to our museum website, and 5,680 organization members.

Mainstreet, Downtown Bozeman

There are 680 people living in downtown Bozeman. 47.8% of these people are milenials. 20.1% of this population work in education or heathcare.

Big Sky Resort

Big Sky is the largest ski resort in the western United States. Opened in late 1973, Big Sky Resort has more than 5,800 acres (9.1 sq mi; 23.5 km2) of terrain and a vertical drop of 4,350 feet (1,330 m).

Cluster Gallatin National Forest

Established in 1899, this 1.8-million acre forest spans six mountain ranges and provides a habitat for a full complement of native fauna, including four federally listed threatened species - the grizzly bear, gray wolf, bald eagle, and the Canada lynx.

Bozeman Hot Springs

These hot springs feature 12 pool ranging from 59-106 degrees. Single time admission prices range from 8-17 dollars.

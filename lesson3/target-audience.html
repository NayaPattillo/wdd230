Target Audience

Home Design

-Description

The target audience of this website are local business owners and community members looking for various resources and information about the community and local economy.

-Two Personas

  • David Reed is a 33 year old small business owner who lives in the area. He has a small family of a wife and two kids. His business is a small book store in the main part of town. He wants to find out the demographic of people who would like to join a book club and where in the community he should advertise. He is looking through the site on his computer at home.
  • Ricky Baker is 25 years old and owns a food truck. He is single and lives in a small apartment with two roomates. He is looking for community gatherings and events that he could cater to. He is browsing through the site on his phone in his truck.

-Five scenarios

  • What events are happening this week and where?
  • Where is the heaviest foot traffic in town?
  • Where can I find marketing opportunities?
  • How do I become a member of the chamber?
  • Is there a program or opportunity that could help my business grow?
